Our Mission

Nivara strives to reduce food waste by connecting those who have excess food with those in need.

We believe that no one should go hungry, and through our platform, we aim to make a difference in the lives of needy individuals and families.

Our initiative allows restaurants, businesses, and individuals to donate surplus food, ensuring that it reaches those who require it the most.

Additionally, we provide an easy way for generous donors to contribute financially, helping us maintain and expand our operations.

Together, we can work towards a hunger-free community and contribute to a sustainable future.
